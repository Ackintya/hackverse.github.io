HackVerse
24-Hour Nation Wide Hackathon

National Institute of Technology Karnataka, Surathkal

Jan 25th - 26th, 2020

What is Hackverse ?

HackVerse serves as a platform to encourage enthusiastic minds to brainstorm on solutions for challenging issues from all over India. The unified motive of gathering all like-minded hackers to our alma mater, is what led to the creation of the idea in NITK Surathkal, the first of its kind. We’ll provide a platform for innovations, where developers can test and showcase their potential to the best of their abilities.

The Institution

National Institute of Technology Karnataka (NITK) is one of the premier engineering institutions of the country. Situated near Mangalore along the coast of the Arabian Sea, NITK is home to some of the brightest young minds of the country engaged in a wide array of disciplines. With a vibrant and robust student body, NITK has been the launchpad for people with great ideas that have grown to be very successful ventures including Practo, Purpule, SaveMonk among others. This year, we celebrate our Diamond Jubilee. To commemorate 60 years of NITK, IE NITK aims to gather all like minded hackers to our own institute.

The Organizers

The Institution of Engineers, NITK Chapter is an exclusive technical club of NITK, aiming to increase technical exposure to all students. Our previous Digital Hackathon which was conducted last year, sparked our ingenious students to transpire cutting edge solutions to various problem statements. Our other events include Spark, a mini TED talk series which has completed nine editions successfully, Enigma, a set of challenges meant to peak technical enjoyment which witnesses over 400 participants and KOTH, an entertainment filled collection of challenges.

FAQs


  • Is this hackathon an online event or offline?

    HackVerse is an offline event and as a participant you need to be present at NITK campus.

  • Is there any registration fee?

    No. Registrations are completely free of cost.

  • Will accommodation and food be provided?

    Yes. Sure. Sí. हाँ. Absolutely. You will be provided with proper meals. Thanks to our sponsors.

  • What is the selection criteria?

    We are looking for people who innovate and can build crazy, unique solutions to real-world problems. Tell us why you want to be a part of HackVerse, any accomplishments you are proud of and anything else which could strengthen your application.

  • How big can a team be?

    You can form teams of up to 3 people. Most teams aim to have a mix of people with both design and developer skills.

  • What should I do if I do not have any team yet?

    Just fill out the application form and we will match you with the best team possible on site. You could also join our Telegram group to find potential teammates.

  • What are the perks of participating in this hackathon?

    The hackathon will provide hands on experience with the experts from different field working on the. Along with that the participant will be provided with cool swag, stickers and refreshments to keep them up. Also our sponsors will be providing problem statements and if team works on the problem statements, they may get company prizes and recruitment benefits.

  • What should I bring?

    Bring your student ID, laptop, chargers. A pillow and sleeping bag may be useful as well. Please leave anything illegal at home.

  • Can I participate without a computer science background?

    Yes. Creativity is independent of your technical skills. You can learn the tools and technologies anytime.

  • Are there any problem statements for the hackathon?

    We believe being forced to build on problem statements limits creativity and participants should have the freedom to express their creativity. However some broad tracks are Artificial Intelligence, Blockchain, FinTech, Future Mobility, Algorithms, AR/VR.

  • Can I start working on my hack before the event?

    No. In the interest of fairness, students should not be working on their projects before the hackathon begins and we do not allow participants to work on pre-existing projects. However, you can familiarize yourself with all the tools and technologies you intend to use beforehand.
